About Us


First person shooter(FPS) games have proven themselves to be one of the most popular genres in video game history. Not only is the gaming community's appetite for unfettered mayhem never ending, but this type of melee has penetrated the consciousness of casual gamers. The commercially successful franchises are now household names: Call of Duty, Doom, Halo, to name just a few.
Elanza is a global entertainment media brand powered by fan passion specially for FPS Gaming enthusiasts. It is one stop for fun, original multi-platform content.
